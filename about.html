  • Paybridge gives healthcare and medical service providers access to cash quickly for invoices that are due to be paid by health insurance companies.

    Managing cash flow is usually a challenge for hospitals and insurers. One of their main sources of revenue is claim payments from medical insurance companies, who often pay slowly. Claims can take 30 to 180 days to pay depending on the negotiated terms. Slow payments have an obvious and negative impact on cash flow.

    For Health Insurers, HMOs operate in a highly competitive and dynamic market. Like many businesses, they face the challenges of managing their cash flow effectively. The primary source of revenue comes from premium payments, but they often come with significant delays. The delay in premium payment can be as long as 6 months with HMOs carrying up to 70% of their booked income as receivables in some cases.

    At Paybridge, we can provide quick access to a range of business funding solutions that are specifically suited to healthcare providers. Whether it’s factoring to turn unpaid invoices into cash or working capital loans to plug short-term gaps in cash flow, we have the funding solutions to help you grow.

Why Paybridge?

  • Improved Cash Flow

    Businesses can access cash quickly by leveraging their accounts receivable, which can be especially beneficial when customers have long payment terms.

  • Working Capital

    Paybridge provides a source of working capital without taking on additional debt or diluting ownership by selling equity.

  • Risk Mitigation

    By converting receivables into cash, businesses can reduce the risk associated with late or non-payment by customers.

  • Simplified Collections and Audit.

    Businesses can focus on their core operations while Paybridge handles the often time-consuming task of auditing and collecting payments from your Insurer

  • Competitive Advantage

    With increased cash flow and reduced risk of payment default, hospitals are well positioned to delivery better services.

  • Cheaper than Bank Loans

    Paybridge is typically less expensive than bank loans, and it is also more straightforward to obtain than bank loans. This will assist in meeting the short-term funding requirements.

  • Unsecured Financing

    Paybridge does not require you to provide collateral, it reduces the danger of losing collateral while also making processing and disbursement more efficient.
